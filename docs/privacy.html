Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Z.E Digital Tech. ("we", "us", "our") operates the ELO-AGI (NEURO) website and associated services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address (when contacting us or subscribing to updates)
  • Payment information (processed by PayPal or your bank; we do not store payment details)
  • Name and organization (for sponsor listings)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)

Interactive Demo Data

Commands entered in the interactive REPL demo may be sent to our API server for processing. These commands are not stored or logged beyond the immediate processing session. No personal data is extracted from demo interactions.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the website and services
  • Process sponsorship payments
  • Display sponsor names/logos as agreed
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send updates about ELO-AGI (only if you opt in)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors (PayPal) for transaction processing
  • GitHub Pages for website hosting
  • Fly.io for API service hosting

These service providers are bound by their own privacy policies and process data only as needed to provide their services.

5. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Any cookies used are strictly necessary for website functionality (such as remembering your preferences).

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Open Source Data

The ELO-AGI framework itself does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. It runs entirely locally on your machine. Any data processed by the framework stays on your local system.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of communications
  • Request your sponsor listing be removed

To exercise these rights, contact elvizekaj@zedigital.tech.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at elvizekaj@zedigital.tech.
